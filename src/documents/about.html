Did you know that computer art is a thing? Dot:Dot is a project Ksenia Karelskaya did for her bachelor proof. She made it for herself and her friends, who were willing to have a simple, minimalistic but unique tattoos. It was also the first tattoo Ksenia got.

 

Generator is written with a help of Processing.js and produces unique values. So no tattoo’s are the same. We’ve consulted with tattoo artists about line width so that you don’t need to correct them every year.

 

You can check out the video beneath about how the project was created, and take a look at the gallery where real tattoo’s are presented.
